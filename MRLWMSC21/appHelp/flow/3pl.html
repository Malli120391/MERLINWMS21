    1. 3PL
      1. Tariff Creation
        1. Tariff Groups

            Link: Menu 3PL Tariff Group

          1. Tariff Groups Are fixed as per the Business Rules (like Handling, Storage... etc.). Other functionality includes search on Tariff groups, Edit and Update the Tariff Groups.
        2. Tariff Sub-Groups

            Link: Menu 3PL Tariff Sub-Group

          1. For each Tariff Group is having different Sub-Groups as per the Business Rules.
          2. (Ex: Handling is having different sub groups like Inbound Order Processing, Unloading ...etc.).
          3. Tariff Sub-Groups are common for both Inbound and Outbound. Other functionality includes search on Tariff Sub-Groups, Edit and Update the Tariff Sub-Groups.
        3. Tariffs

            Link: Menu 3PL Tariffs

          1. Tariffs are used for creating the Unit Cost and Effective date of that particular Tariff Sub-Group (From, To). Tenant Info displays the charge applied to the Tenant for the particular Sub-Group. Other functionality includes search on Tariff Sub-Groups, Edit, Update, Delete the Tariff Sub-Groups.
      2. Tariff Allocation

        Link: Menu 3PL Tariff Allocation

        1. Tariff Allocation is used for allocating the Tariff to the Tenant with Unit cost, Discount and Effective dates. Other functionality includes search on Tenant, Edit, Update and Delete the Tenant allocation details.

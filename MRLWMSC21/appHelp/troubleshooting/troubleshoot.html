    • Why am I unable to upload the invoice sent by the vendor/supplier into the PO ?
      • You should only upload the invoices in the standard ASN Invoice launch format provided by the system.
    • Where do I find the Standard ASN Invoice format ?
      • In Inbound keyboard_arrow_left ASN Import,launch a “Get Template” pic button is provided. When clicked on it, the standard ASN Invoice format in an Excel sheet is downloaded.
    • Though the ASN template is imported, “Create Inbound”launch cannot be updated successfully. Why?

        Probability of reasons could be the following:

      • All the Mandatory fields are not given
      • Any special characters are included in the excel
      • PO number is not configured in FWMS
    • After “Create Inbound”, can the ASN launch be edited ?
      • No, because two invoices cannot have the same PO.
    • How to modify the ASNlaunch ?
      • Go to Inbound Searchlaunch, select the invoice and click on Modify icon. This can be done until the Inbound is closed.
    • Will I be able to receive partial shipments from the vendors in this WMS ?
      • Yes! The vendor can send a single Purchase Order (PO) in multiple Inbounds.
    • Can I map one ASNlaunch with multiple purchase orders ?
      • Yes! You can map one ASNlaunch with multiple purchase orders but it must be from the same supplier.
    • Why isn’t the system allowing dock allocation for material Inward?

        Probability of reasons could be the following:

      • NO docks are created for the respective warehouse.
      • Available docks are already in use for other vehicles.
      • While adding a Dock, if the “Est. Dock-In Time” deceeded the current time.
    • How to check if the any docks are created for a warehouse or not ?
      • In Yard Management keyboard_arrow_left Gate Entry,launch after submitting the “Account &Vehicle Info” and “Shipment Info”, “Docking Info” panel is displayed. Click on “Add Docks +” and check the Dock # dropdown after giving the Supplier, Invoice and Shipment Ref #. If an empty dropdown is displayed that indicates there are no docks created for the warehouse.
    • How to create Docks for a warehouse ?
      • In Master Data keyboard_arrow_left Account,launch go to warehouse details panel and in the below list click on the edit icon for the selected warehouse. Go to dock creation panel and click on “Create Dock”. A popup will be displayed to create a dock. Only the warehouse manager can create the dock.
    • How to check the Dock availability or are allocated for other vehicles ?
      • In Yard Management keyboard_arrow_left Gate Entry,launch after submitting the “Account &Vehicle Info” and “Shipment Info”, “Docking Info” panel is displayed. Click on “Add Docks +” and check the Dock # dropdown after giving the Supplier, Invoice and Shipment Ref #. If “Est. Dock In Time” is displayed that means the dock is allocated to a vehicle/s and if the “Est. Dock In Time” is not displayed that means the dock is available and no vehicle is allocated to it.
    • Where to check if the “Est. Dock-In Time” deceeded the current time or not ?
      • In Yard Management keyboard_arrow_left Gate Entry,launch after submitting the “Account &Vehicle Info” and “Shipment Info”, “Docking Info” panel is displayed. Click on “Add Docks +” and check the Dock # dropdown after giving the Supplier, Invoice and Shipment Ref #. If “Est. Dock In Time” given by the user is prior to the current time, then an alert message is thrown saying “Est. Dock In Time Should Not Deceeds Current Time”.
    • An already added Dock is edited and its Invoice number is changed and clicked on “Save”. An error message is shown as “Invoice is already added”. Why ?
      • When an Invoice is changed, its respective shipment ref # must also be reselected from the fetched list.
    • Why haven’t the system suggested a location for putaway ?

        Probability of reasons could be the following:

      • Material dimensions are not configured in the item mater.
      • Material category is not configured in the item master.
      • Bin Dimensions are not configured in location master.
    • Where to configure Material dimensions ?
      • Material Dimensions must be fetched from SAP; there is no updation in FWMS.
    • Where to configure Material category ?
      • Material category must be fetched from SAP; there is no updation in FWMS.
    • Where to configure Bin Dimensions ?
      • Bin Dimensions must be fetched from SAP; there is no updation in FWMS.
    • Will I be able to override the system suggested putaway ?
      • Yes! This happens only when the system logically shows bin availability but physical that bin is full. Here the system mandates to update the reason for such actions in handheld putaway screen and eventually a Cycle count will be triggered.
    • Why GRNlaunch is not showing as updated ?
      • This can be because receiving of materials is not completed as per the shipment.
